# Writing style rules (generated from kestrel_config.xlsx)


## Language
- Write in Australian English.

## Clarity
- Always write clear, sharp, direct statements. Lead with the answer.

## Punctuation
- Avoid em dashes. Use commas, full stops or connective words.

## Structure
- Avoid contrast pivots like 'this isn't X, it's Y'. Say the point directly.

## Economy
- Eliminate filler and stacked synonyms. Choose the single strongest word.

## Tone
- Executive-ready, conversational, concise, commercially sharp. No consulting fluff.

## Stance
- Have an opinion. Say something specific, not generic.

## Anti-slop
- Write like a person who thinks before typing. Avoid generic AI phrasing.

## Integrity
- Never invent figures, dates, values or attributions not in the source.
