# Kestrel brand pack

This zip contains a refined starter pack for the approved **primary line-art Kestrel** direction, plus premium and modern variants.

## Included
- Transparent-background logo assets, light and dark
- Premium variant, light and dark
- Modern variant, light and dark
- Small-format icons for Outlook and Teams tiles in 64, 128, 192, 256 and 512 px
- Light and dark masthead/header lock-ups
- One-page usage rules PDF
- Preview sheet

## Recommended defaults
- **Primary light transparent** for standard light backgrounds
- **Primary dark transparent** for dark mode or dark backgrounds
- **Outlook header 600x200** for email mastheads
- **192 px icon tile** for Teams or SharePoint tiles

## Notes
- Keep the sub-heading locked as **D&DI market scan**
- Use the premium and modern variants sparingly
- This is a concept pack for internal alignment and fast rollout
